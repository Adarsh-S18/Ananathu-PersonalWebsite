About Me

I'm Ananthu Vasudev - a neurodivergent creative navigating life as an exhilarating roller coaster. My compass points toward three things: self-worth, creativity, and social impact. Everything I do flows through this lens, and I refuse to do anything the ordinary way.

Over 15 years in operations and general management, I've shaped organizations across education, healthcare, and beyond. I co-founded The Bridge Business Solutions, steering it through growth until my exit in 2023, then birthed The Evolvers Project in 2020. Today, I consult across sectors and mentor professionals through their career transitions. My decade-long recruitment journey led me to pen "The Ultimate Resume Guide," and I'm honored as a LinkedIn Top Voice - recognized among 1 billion global users.

My real passion lies in creating ripples of change. I've founded NGOs and collaborated with movements like Manass, ANTS, The Peace Movement, Say No To Harthal, and Kottayam Koode. In 2014, I coordinated Kochi's first ever stem cell collection camp - a project that continues saving lives. Since 2012, I've worked with the Government High School for the Blind, designing creative initiatives that celebrate and empower visually impaired students.

I'm fueled by strong coffee and comedy, drawn to gadgets and off-road adventures, and perpetually intrigued by people and their stories. My ADHD, anxiety, and depression episodes transformed into my greatest teachers, making me a devoted mental health advocate. I listen deeply; because every story matters, every life matters.
